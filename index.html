mtg.ai



This is a project I started with two other people.
Today's paradigm of cloud-based SaaS apps creates more data in more places without a single source of truth. Even worse, talking in person makes everything digital outdated. So we created Aviva, a digital assistant for teams who uses machine learning to organize and distill spoken and written business communication to keep teams in the loop.

Audio summarization tool



This is a tool I created which receives snippets of audio and displays a beautiful and intuitive UI for the user allowing her to play/pause, skip and visually label bits of audio. The user is then able to summarize only the importants parts of the audio and subsequently share their notes.

Gazelle.vision



I spent 10 months working with the Gazelle.vision team building a beautiful and easy to use UI for their clients. The product automates a portion of the lead generation business and puts more power into the client's hands by giving them the ability to use their profile to find likely matches and make initial contact with said matches sooner. I learned a great deal about front-end development during this period and I lucky to be working with some of the great technologies that are reshaping the way front-ends are designed, namely React and Redux.
